Frequently Asked Questions

🎂 Do you make custom cakes?

Yes! We specialize in custom cakes for all occasions. You can place an order through our contact page or visit us in-store.

📅 How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend placing your order at least 3–5 days in advance. For wedding cakes, at least 2 weeks' notice is ideal.

🚚 Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we deliver within Lusaka! Delivery charges vary based on the distance. Contact us for a quote.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money, cash, and card payments. Details will be provided during order confirmation.

🍰 Do you offer eggless or gluten-free cakes?

Yes, we do! Please let us know your dietary preferences when placing the order.
